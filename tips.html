Tips for Seniors Using Technology

  • Take your time learning — don’t rush through new apps or websites.
  • Use large fonts and high-contrast themes for better visibility.
  • Keep your passwords safe and use strong, unique passwords.
  • Be cautious with emails or messages from unknown sources.
  • Ask family or friends for help if you get stuck — it’s okay to learn together.
  • Regularly update apps and devices to ensure security.
